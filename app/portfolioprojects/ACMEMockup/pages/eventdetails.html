Stockholm International Horse Show is an international, annual equistrian event held in Stockholm. The show has a unique mix of the best equestrian sport with the biggest international stars in competition and show. This is one of the world's largest indoor events in equestrian sports in terms of the number of visitors per day and is one of Stockholm's largest recurring events. The event is divided into 3 different shows. Event Details 1. International Jumping- This is an event where the world’s best riders compete. Ten international and ten Swedish riders meet in various jumping events. 2. International Shows- This is where the international artists, along with their horses perform fabulous shows. 3. Exhibition- The is a judged exhibition of horses of different breeds and equestrian discipline
